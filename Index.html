How to Create a Roblox Gamepass

  1. Go to Roblox Develop Page.
  2. Select a game and click 'Store'.
  3. Click 'Create a Pass' and upload an image.
  4. Set a name and description, then click 'Create'.
  5. Configure the pass, set it for sale, and set a price.

How to Invite Your Friends

  1. Join our Discord server: PantherHub.
  2. Go to the invite section in Discord.
  3. Click 'Create Invite' and copy the link.
  4. Share the link with your friends.
  5. Once they join, let an admin know for verification.

Thank you for participating! Your reward will be processed soon.
